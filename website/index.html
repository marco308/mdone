Everything you need

A focused home for the work that matters.

mDone brings your Vikunja tasks to life with the native iOS and macOS touches you'd expect, and nothing you wouldn't.

Smart Lists

Today, Upcoming, and Overdue views organise everything automatically, so you always know what to focus on next.

Projects & Favorites

Organise tasks into projects and pin the ones you live in to the sidebar for one-tap access.

Current

Pin long-running tasks to a section above Today, each with a progress bar and an idle nudge when one stalls, so slow-burn projects actually get finished.

Home & Lock Screen Widgets

Today's agenda, upcoming, and overdue, right on your home or lock screen. Customise size and content.

Focus Timer

Work through tasks with purpose. Live Activities keep the timer on your lock screen and Dynamic Island.

Repeating Tasks

Daily, weekly, monthly or custom intervals. Completed repeats generate their next occurrence automatically.

Calendar View

See your tasks across days and weeks. Spot the gaps, plan ahead, and keep your week in balance.

Offline Support

Tasks are cached locally so you can keep working without a connection. Changes sync when you're back online.

Stay on top of it

Today, front and centre.

Open mDone and the right things are already in front of you. Smart lists pull your day together so nothing slips.

  • Quick-add a task without leaving the list
  • Swipe to complete, postpone, or reschedule
  • Shake to undo an accidental completion
See the week

The shape of your time.

An integrated calendar lays your tasks out across days and weeks, and folds in your device calendar events so you see the whole picture.

  • Tasks and calendar events, side by side
  • Choose which calendars contribute
  • Start the week on any day you like
Every detail

Rich tasks, one tap away.

Set due dates, priorities, repeats, estimates, and Markdown notes in a detail view that feels native on every screen.

  • Markdown descriptions render beautifully
  • Add a focus session right from a task
  • Estimate how long it'll take
Right at home on the Mac

A real macOS app, not a port.

A proper sidebar layout, keyboard-friendly navigation, and the same data, synced through your own server.

Focus

One task. The clock. Nothing else.

Start a focus session on any task and the rest fades away. A Live Activity keeps the timer on your Lock Screen and in the Dynamic Island, so you can set the phone down and keep going.

  • Pause, resume, or finish without unlocking
  • Live Activity on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island
  • Focus time and session counts saved per task
Home & Lock Screen

Your day, before you open the app.

Today, upcoming, and overdue right where you glance. Long-press to choose the size, what it shows, and the text scale.

Privacy isn't a feature

It's the whole point.

Self-hosting means your data is yours. mDone respects that completely. It talks only to the Vikunja server you choose, and nothing else.

Zero analytics

No telemetry, no usage tracking, no "anonymous" data collection. None.

No third-party SDKs

Nothing bundled to phone home. Just your device and your server.

Credentials in the Keychain

Your token is stored securely on-device, never sent anywhere else.

Open source

Built in the open, MIT-licensed.

Pure Swift and SwiftUI, no external dependencies. Read the code, file an issue, or send a pull request. It's all on GitHub.
